# **App Name**: AetherAI

## Core Features:

- Homepage Hero Section: Visually striking hero section with a gradient background (Violet to Blue) and a headline that reads 'Building Intelligent AI Products for the Future'.
- Product Showcase: Display AI products with large visuals and brief descriptions, accessible through horizontal scrolling service cards.
- Industry Solutions: Present various industries served using logo-style cards that navigate to specific solutions pages.
- Case Study Highlights: Feature compelling case studies with visual elements, showcasing successful AI implementations.
- Careers Section: Friendly and energetic careers section with engaging illustrations to attract potential talent.
- Insights Blog: Modern blog layout for sharing insights, trends, and thought leadership pieces related to AI.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Violet (#8b5cf6) to represent innovation and modernity.
- Secondary color: Blue (#3b82f6) to convey trust and stability.
- CTA color: Orange (#f97316) to draw attention to calls to action and important links.
- Use rounded cards for a modern, soft, and approachable design.
- Incorporate soft gradients to add depth and visual appeal.
- Implement subtle animations and hover effects to enhance user engagement and provide feedback.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' sans-serif for a clean and modern aesthetic. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
